The end of "The Grand Tour"

Posted on February 6, 2024

Amazon Prime have released the last season of The Grand Tour, the "Sand Job". The last chapter, in which Jeremy Clarckson, James May and Richard Hammond travel to Zimbabue, will arrive to Spain at the end of this year. I am very excited to see the last adventure of these three friends, but it is a shame that after so many years, laughts and extraordinary cars testings, the Zimbabue special chapter will close that legendary series for ever.
I am looking forward to see what the future holds them, and I am sure that, as well as me, most of you will not never forgive the epic trip these presenters along Top Gear and Grand Tour.
